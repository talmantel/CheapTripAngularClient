Clink78 Hostel

Clink78 Hostel is a great choice for budget travellers looking for a unique and affordable accommodation option. Located in a former courthouse in the trendy neighbourhood of King's Cross, this hostel offers comfortable dormitory rooms and private rooms at budget-friendly prices. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, a self-catering kitchen, and a lively bar on site. Perfect for budget-conscious travellers looking for a unique and central location.
