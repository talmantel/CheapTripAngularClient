Aalborg Opera Festival - Experience the Beauty of Opera!

Experience the beauty of opera at Aalborg Opera Festival! This free event features performances by local and international opera singers. It's the perfect event for budget-conscious travelers who want to experience the local culture and enjoy some amazing music. Don't miss out on this wonderful event!

Aalborg Opera Festival takes place every year in August. Here opera virgins and opera fans meet with tourists and students and there is plenty to choose from. We play free street opera, show opera films, sing the sun down and present some of the biggest stars of our time.
